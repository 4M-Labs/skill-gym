## Why This Works

- **Ultra-brief** -- 4 sentences. C-suite respects brevity.
- **Peer-level tone** -- No salesy language. Just a direct question.
- **Social proof** -- FastFreight comparison is close in size, making it credible and relatable.
- **Low-friction CTA** -- "10-minute call" is easy to say yes to.
- **No jargon** -- Avoids "AI-powered SaaS platform" or similar buzzwords that feel out of touch with traditional logistics leaders.
