## Tone Notes

- **Warm, not cutesy.** We're talking to adults making a real decision about their pet's safety.
- **Specific over superlative.** "Background checked" beats "the best." "Video meet-and-greet" beats "trusted."
- **Empathy first, features second.** Lead with the guilt, the worry, the desire for something better — then show how we solve it.
- **Never disparage kennels outright.** Many readers have used them. Don't make them feel stupid. Just show them the upgrade.
