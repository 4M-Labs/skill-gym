## Why This Works

| Principle | Application |
|---|---|
| **Peer-level voice** | No jargon, no pitch-deck language. Reads like a colleague sharing a relevant data point. |
| **Ultra-brief** | 4 sentences total. C-suite doesn't read long emails. |
| **Lead with their world** | Opens with the problem they live daily, not who you are. |
| **Proof, not features** | One concrete result from a comparable org. That's the whole pitch. |
| **Low-friction CTA** | "Worth a 10-minute look?" — easy yes, no calendar commitment. |
| **No fake personalization** | The personalization is structural (C-suite, enterprise buyer, healthcare) not surface-level ("I saw your LinkedIn post"). |
| **Respects the sales cycle** | Doesn't push for a demo or meeting. Just asks if it's worth exploring. |
