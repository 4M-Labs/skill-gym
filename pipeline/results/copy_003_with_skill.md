### Objection Handling

**Section Header**  
Questions We Hear From Teams Comparing Tools

**"We already use Canva. Why switch?"**  
Canva works well for individual creators. DesignFlow is built for teams that need brand control, approval workflows, and shared assets. If you have more than 5 people creating content, you will feel the limits of Canva fast.

**"Figma is more powerful. Why not use that?"**  
Figma is excellent for product design teams. DesignFlow is built for marketing teams — simpler to learn, faster to onboard, and designed around the content you actually produce: social posts, ads, presentations, and print materials.

**"What if we outgrow Starter?"**  
Upgrade any time from your dashboard. Your existing projects, templates, and brand assets carry over — nothing gets lost.

**"Is there a learning curve?"**  
If you have used Canva, you will feel at home within minutes. DesignFlow is intentionally simpler than Figma because marketing teams need speed, not a design tool that requires training.

**"What happens after the 14-day trial?"**  
You choose a plan. If you do not upgrade, your account pauses — no charges, no data loss. Come back whenever you are ready.
