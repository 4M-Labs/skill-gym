Congrats on the SOC 2 certification — most series B fintechs push that to series C, so clearly it mattered to you.

The part nobody warns you about: the audit is a snapshot, not a culture. Growing from 30 to 50 engineers is exactly when security knowledge gaps turn into incidents. New hires bring habits from previous companies, and "someone will catch it" stops working at that size.

We worked with PaySecure (250-person fintech, also Series B) right after their SOC 2. Within 6 months their security incidents dropped 40% — not from more tooling, but from giving their team the training to spot problems before they become tickets.
