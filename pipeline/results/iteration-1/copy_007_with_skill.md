# FreshBooks Cloud — CTA Copy (Iteration 1)

---

## All 7 CTAs with Page Context

### 1. Homepage Hero
**CTA:** `Start Your Free 30-Day Trial — No Credit Card Required`

**Context:** First impression. Visitor is evaluating whether this tool fits them. The hero CTA must reduce friction and remove the #1 fear (getting charged without knowing).

---

### 2. Homepage Bottom (Footer CTA)
**CTA:** `Get Back to the Work You Love`

**Context:** This visitor scrolled the entire homepage. They know what FreshBooks does. The closing CTA should be emotional — tie the product to the outcome they actually care about (doing their real work, not bookkeeping).

---

### 3. Features Page
**CTA:** `See How FreshBooks Handles Your Invoicing`

**Context:** Visitor is on the Features page comparing capabilities. This CTA is contextual — it references a specific feature (invoicing) that is the core pain point for freelancers. It invites exploration rather than hard conversion.

---

### 4. Pricing Page — Starter Tier
**CTA:** `Start Free for 30 Days`

**Context:** Price-sensitive freelancer comparing options. The lowest tier CTA emphasizes free trial and zero commitment. No qualifiers needed — the tier itself signals affordability.

---

### 5. Pricing Page — Business Tier
**CTA:** `Get the Full Accounting Suite`

**Context:** The mid-tier visitor is likely a freelancer ready to scale. "Full accounting suite" signals completeness and value without the enterprise-speak that alienates solo operators.

---

### 6. Pricing Page — Premium Tier
**CTA:** `See If Premium Fits My Practice`

**Context:** Premium targets established consultants with higher revenue. The phrasing "fits my practice" respects their identity as a professional business, not just a freelancer. The softer "see if" invites evaluation without pressure.

---

### 7. Case Studies Page
**CTA:** `Read How Freelancers Like Me Saved 10+ Hours a Month`

**Context:** Social proof page. The visitor wants validation that others like them had success. "Like me" personalizes the CTA. "Saved 10+ Hours a Month" is a specific, tangible outcome that makes the value concrete.

---

### 8. About Page
**CTA:** `Join 30 Million Small Businesses Who Trust FreshBooks`

**Context:** About page visitors care about credibility and values. The CTA uses social proof (30M users) and frames joining as belonging to a community, not just buying software.

---

### 9. Contact Page
**CTA:** `Get an Answer From a Real Human`

**Context:** Contact page visitor is stuck or has a specific question. "Real human" differentiates from chatbot frustration and reassures them they'll get a genuine response. It's warm and on-brand.

---

## Annotations: Why Each CTA Was Chosen

| # | Page | CTA | Formula Used | Why It Works |
|---|------|-----|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | Homepage Hero | Start Your Free 30-Day Trial — No Credit Card Required | [Action] + [What They Get] + [Friction Remover] | Removes the biggest objection (payment info) upfront. "Free 30-Day Trial" is specific and sets expectations. |
| 2 | Homepage Bottom | Get Back to the Work You Love | [Action] + [Emotional Outcome] | Closes with the real motivation — freelancers don't want to do accounting, they want to do their craft. Emotional pull beats feature list at the bottom of a page. |
| 3 | Features Page | See How FreshBooks Handles Your Invoicing | [Action] + [Specific Feature] | References the #1 feature freelancers search for. "See how" invites exploration, which is what a features-page visitor wants — not a hard sell. |
| 4 | Pricing — Starter | Start Free for 30 Days | [Action] + [Zero-Cost Qualifier] | Simple and direct for the budget-conscious visitor. No fluff, no ambiguity. The word "Free" does the heavy lifting. |
| 5 | Pricing — Business | Get the Full Accounting Suite | [Action] + [Value Proposition] | "Full accounting suite" elevates the product perception without corporate jargon. "Get" is strong and direct. |
| 6 | Pricing — Premium | See If Premium Fits My Practice | [Action] + [Evaluation Framing] | Low-pressure framing for a high-consideration purchase. "My practice" mirrors how established consultants see themselves. |
| 7 | Case Studies | Read How Freelancers Like Me Saved 10+ Hours a Month | [Action] + [Social Proof + Specific Metric] | Combines social proof ("freelancers like me") with a hard number. Specificity beats vague promises. |
| 8 | About | Join 30 Million Small Businesses Who Trust FreshBooks | [Action] + [Social Proof + Community] | "Join" implies belonging, not transacting. The 30M number is a credibility bomb that eliminates trust concerns. |
| 9 | Contact | Get an Answer From a Real Human | [Action] + [Differentiator] | "Real human" is the play. Everyone hates chatbot loops. This signals warmth and actual service — perfectly on-brand for a friendly voice. |

---

**CTAs avoided:** Submit, Sign Up, Learn More, Click Here, Get Started
**Voice consistency:** Friendly, reassuring, slightly playful — no corporate stiffness, no hype.
