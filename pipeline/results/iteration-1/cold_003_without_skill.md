## Sequence Notes

| Email | Timing | Purpose |
|-------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Day 3 (after initial) | Re-engage, anchor to hiring trigger |
| 2 | Day 8 | Create urgency with a question |
| 3 | Day 15 | Respectful close, low-pressure exit |

**Key Principles:**
- Reference specific signals (5 job postings) — shows you did your homework
- Keep emails under 100 words — respect the reader's time
- Each email stands alone — no assumption the previous was read
- P.S. in Email 3 provides a passive conversion path
- Never use "just following up" or "checking in" — offer value each time
