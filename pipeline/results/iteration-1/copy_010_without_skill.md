## Notes on Voice & Strategy

- **Voice:** Warm, reassuring, trustworthy. Reads like advice from a friend who gets it, not a brand trying to sell.
- **Differentiator emphasis:** Background checks + pet first aid certification + video meet-and-greets. This is the trust trifecta that separates PetPal from Craigslist sitters and impersonal kennels.
- **Pain point:** Guilt. The 25-40 professional who loves their pet but can't bring them to every conference. They don't need a pitch — they need permission to trust someone else.
- **Social proof lever:** "Stopped using kennels" reframes the decision as an upgrade, not a splurge.
