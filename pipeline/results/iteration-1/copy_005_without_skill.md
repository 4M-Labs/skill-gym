## Body Copy

You spend hours every week deciding what to work on next. Dragging tasks around boards. Reassigning deadlines. Moving the same card from "In Progress" to "Blocked" three times in a week.

TaskPilot reads your existing project data — from Asana, Trello, or both — and surfaces what actually matters right now. It learns how long tasks really take based on your team's history, flags work that's likely to slip, and suggests priorities so you don't have to start each morning with a triage meeting.

No new workflow to learn. No complex setup. Connect your current tools and let TaskPilot work alongside the systems your team already uses.

**What changes when you stop prioritizing manually:**

- Fewer tasks fall through the cracks — TaskPilot catches what slips past your current process
- Your team spends less time in status meetings and more time on actual deliverables
- New team members get oriented faster because priorities are already clear
