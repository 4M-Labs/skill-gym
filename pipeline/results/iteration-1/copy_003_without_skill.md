## Why Teams Switch to DesignFlow

### vs. Canva
Canva is great for individual creators. DesignFlow adds the team features Canva lacks: approval workflows, brand kit enforcement, and role-based access controls.

### vs. Figma
Figma is a powerhouse for product designers. DesignFlow is purpose-built for marketing teams who need templates, brand consistency, and non-designer-friendly workflows.

### vs. Adobe Express
Adobe Express offers solid templates. DesignFlow pairs that with real collaboration features so your team isn't emailing files back and forth.
