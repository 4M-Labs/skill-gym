**Placement:** Bottom of every blog post (SEO, paid ads, content marketing articles)
**Target audience:** Marketing managers at B2B SaaS companies
**Voice:** Professional, authoritative, value-first

## Implementation Notes

- **Headline formula:** Specificity + relevance beats generic urgency. "2,400+ B2B SaaS accounts" anchors authority. "Actually use" implies筛选筛选 (editorial curation).
- **CTA language:** First-person framing ("Join 12,000 marketing managers") outperforms imperative ("Subscribe now") in B2B contexts because it frames membership, not obligation.
- **Micro-copy:** "Free. Unsubscribe anytime" reduces friction. Specifying delivery day ("Tuesday") sets expectations and builds habit.
- **Avoid:** "Newsletter" (low-value connotation), "Updates" (vague), "Don't miss out" (fear-based, misaligned with professional voice).
- **Testing recommendation:** A/B test the primary headline against Version A (problem-agitate) for 2 weeks at 50/50 traffic split. Measure submit rate, not just click rate.

---

## Placement Specifications

- Position: After final article paragraph, before footer
- Visual treatment: Distinct background (light gray or branded accent), contained card layout
- Mobile: Full-width, sticky bottom bar on scroll past 80% of article
- Frequency cap: Show once per session maximum to avoid fatigue
