**Stop Guessing What Your Team Should Work on Next**

## Body Copy

Your project managers spend the first hour of every day reordering boards. Moving deadlines. Asking the same questions in stand-up: What's urgent? What's blocked? What's actually going to ship this week?

TaskPilot connects to Asana or Trello and learns from your real project data. It looks at how your team actually works — which tasks take longer than expected, which deadlines keep slipping, which work depends on something that hasn't started yet — and tells you what to prioritize before your next meeting.

You don't need to change how your team works. TaskPilot sits on top of your current setup and surfaces the priorities your board isn't showing you.

**What your team gets back:**

- Hours per week that used to go to manual reordering and status check-ins
- Fewer missed deadlines because TaskPilot flags at-risk work early
- Clear priorities that new hires can understand without a walkthrough
- Fewer meetings — the data replaces the conversations your board should be having
