## Sequence Notes

**Cadence logic:**
- Email 2 (Day 6): Short, value-driven, references the trigger event directly
- Email 3 (Day 12): Leads with social proof and a concrete result
- Email 4 (Day 21): Breakup pattern creates urgency through loss aversion

**Subject line strategy:**
- All personalized and specific to WorkNest/Priya
- No spam triggers (no "quick question", no RE:, no FW:)
- Each offers a reason to open

**Email structure principles:**
- Under 100 words per email (mobile-friendly)
- Single CTA: 15-minute call
- No attachments or links in first follow-ups
- Personalized opening referencing the trigger event
- Brief pain point articulation
- Clear next step

**Tone:** Professional, consultative, never salesy. Respects her time.

**Key personalization points:**
- Name and title (Priya, Head of People)
- Company name (WorkNest)
- Company size (120 people, remote-first)
- Trigger event (5 job openings on LinkedIn)

**Timing:** All sends during business hours, Tuesday-Thursday preferred.
