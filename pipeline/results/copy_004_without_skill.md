## Key Messaging Pillars

- **Awareness without fear:** The audience knows spreadsheets are problematic. Meet them there.
- **Right-sized solution:** Not enterprise bloat. Not consumer-grade. Built for 10-50 person teams.
- **Easy transition:** Emphasize quick setup and low friction.
- **Concrete value:** Encryption, auto-fill, breach alerts, team sharing. No abstract promises.

## Notes on Voice

- Confident without bragging
- Straightforward without being blunt
- Addresses the real situation: they know it's bad but haven't switched yet
- No fear tactics, no "your business is doomed" messaging
- Focus on the practical upgrade, not the emotional sell
