- **Friendly** — conversational, like a helpful friend who happens to be great with numbers
- **Reassuring** — removes anxiety, builds confidence, never talks down
- **Slightly playful** — light humor allowed, but never flippant about money

## Cross-Page Consistency Notes

- **Tone check:** All CTAs should feel like a helpful nudge, not a hard sell
- **Button language:** Action-oriented, first person preferred ("Start my trial" over "Sign up")
- **Reassurance cues:** Mention free trials, no credit card, no contracts where appropriate
- **Friction reducers:** Time estimates ("2 minutes"), volume proof ("10,000+ freelancers"), cancellation ease ("cancel anytime")
