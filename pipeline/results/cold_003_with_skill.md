## Sequence Notes

| Email | Gap | Purpose |
|-------|-----|---------|
| 1 | Day 0 | Problem-aware, low-friction ask |
| 2 | Day 5 | Data + consequence + deeper value |
| 3 | Day 13 | Breakup with specific pain points + graceful exit |

**Personalization hooks used:**
- 5 job openings (trigger event from LinkedIn)
- Remote-first structure (company-wide context)
- Head of People responsibilities (role-specific pain)

**Skill principles followed:**
- Subject lines: 2-4 words, lowercase, no punctuation tricks
- Opens with their situation, not our product
- No filler phrases ("I hope this finds you well," "I came across your profile")
- Each email stands alone (could be the first email they read)
- Breakup email adds specific value before exit -- not "just checking in"
- Single ask per email (reply or meeting)
- Conversational voice, reads like a peer
- Every sentence earns its place -- no padding
